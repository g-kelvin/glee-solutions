About Us

Glee Hygiene Soutions Ltd, we are a commercial cleaning services and hygiene solutions company and we provide a wide range of services to suit your budgets, needs, and tastes. Every service is customized according to our customer's specific wish list of cleaning needs, requirements, and requested attention.
