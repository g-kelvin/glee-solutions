SPECIFICATIONS

Glee Hygiene Solutions Ltd, with its daily contract cleaning services & hygiene solutions, offers clients the convenience of a full-spectrum of services which range from hard floor care to more specialized forms of cleaning, enabling you as our clients to focus on your core business, while we get on with what we do best.
Our knowledgeable and well-trained team are responsible for cleaning and maintaining a wide range of environments which are:

  • Commercial Cleaning– cleaning of business complexes & buildings, banks & financial institutions, hospitals & medical Centres, schools & educational institutions, shopping malls & centers
