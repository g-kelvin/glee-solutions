At Glee Hygiene Solutions, we recognize that our services are a direct reflection on clients image and for this important reason; we will ensure that we perfectly display the clear picture of cleanliness that the premises should posses
Our objective is to accommodate all our clients cleaning needs. Our cleaning expertise and labor management techniques can provide a variety of options for their customized cleaning schedule. We have established a reputation in the cleaning industry for quality and dependability for our clients entire cleaning requirements. We are focused on the job at hand and we would like to do it with satisfaction.
Based on our experience in the cleaning industry we know that quality control, is the key element to customer satisfaction. In light of the need to uphold the very vital environment care recognition, we religiously adhere to the importance of the quality of service that we provide, comprehensive services with a personalized touch and all must be in line of other quality cleaning concepts.

QUALITY POLICY

  • All personnel are responsible for compliance with the quality management system to ensure that all procedures and work instructions are properly executed and that standards are exceeded.
  • All personnel are keenly trained in the specific duties assigned.
  • The adoption of the quality management system is essential to the continued growth and success of Glee Hygiene and Cleaning Solutions.
